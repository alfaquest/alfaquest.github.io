ΑLΦΑQUΕSΤ GAME NOTES - Premier League winning player surnames

All player names are the most typical versions used in England.
- No international letters, accents, diacritics or fadas are used in this game. In cases where there is a player name which includes such a letter, the closest English alphabetic equivalent is used.
- All player names are case sensitive and include spaces where applicable.
- Also surname prefixes such as "van" for example, where required are case sensitive.
- Entering a different starting letter to what is specified will terminate game.
- Please observe next starting letter dialogs throughout the game.
- Letters "Q" and "X" are ignored in the game, as are all punctuation marks wherever they may occur.
